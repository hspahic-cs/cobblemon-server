# Cobbleworkers

Put your Pokémon to work. Park one on a Pasture Block and it'll auto-harvest, mine, or generate stuff for you while you're off doing other things.

## Jobs

| Job | What it does | Pokémon that can do it |
|---|---|---|
| **Crop Harvester** | Breaks and replants ripe wheat, carrots, potatoes, beetroot | Leafeon, Bellossom, Sunflora, Lilligant, Vileplume, Victreebel, Exeggutor, Roserade, Simisage, Whimsicott |
| **Berry Harvester** | Picks ripe Cobblemon berries | Leafeon, Bellossom, Sunflora, Lilligant, Vileplume, Victreebel, Exeggutor, Roserade, Simisage, Whimsicott |
| **Apricorn Harvester** | Picks ripe apricorns | Vikavolt, Scizor |
| **Mint Harvester** | Picks ripe mints | Clefable, Wigglytuff, Togekiss, Florges, Whimsicott, Slurpuff, Aromatisse, Ninetales |
| **Nether Wart Harvester** | Harvests ripe nether wart | Mismagius, Chandelure, Aegislash, Froslass, Dusknoir, Polteageist |
| **Amethyst Harvester** | Mines ripe amethyst clusters | Rhyperior |
| **Tumblestone Harvester** | Mines tumblestone clusters | Aegislash, Sandslash, Scizor, Steelix |
| **Black Tumblestone Harvester** | Mines black tumblestone | Aegislash, Sandslash, Scizor, Steelix |
| **Sky Tumblestone Harvester** | Mines sky tumblestone | Aegislash, Sandslash, Scizor, Steelix |
| **Fletcher** | Crafts arrows from sticks and feathers in the chest | Vileplume, Victreebel, Roserade, Nidoking, Nidoqueen |
| **Irrigator** | Bonemeals adjacent farmland | Vaporeon, Starmie, Cloyster, Poliwrath, Ludicolo, Simipour, Politoed, Slowking, Kingdra |
| **Extinguisher** | Puts out nearby fires | Vaporeon, Starmie, Cloyster, Poliwrath, Ludicolo, Simipour, Politoed, Slowking, Kingdra |
| **Water Generator** | Fills empty cauldrons with water | Vaporeon, Starmie, Cloyster, Poliwrath, Ludicolo, Simipour, Politoed, Slowking, Kingdra |
| **Lava Generator** | Fills empty cauldrons with lava | Flareon, Arcanine, Ninetales, Simisear, Magmortar |
| **Fuel Generator** | Tops up adjacent furnaces with fuel | Flareon, Arcanine, Ninetales, Simisear, Magmortar |
| **Brewing Fuel Generator** | Tops up adjacent brewing stands with blaze powder | Kingdra |
| **Powder Snow Generator** | Fills empty cauldrons with powder snow | Glaceon, Cetitan, Froslass, Cloyster, Ninetales, Sandslash, Darmanitan |
| **Item Gatherer** | Sweeps up nearby dropped items into the chest | Starmie, Exeggutor, Gallade, Musharna, Slowking |

## Setup

1. Place a **Pasture Block** (you get your first from the *Sunny Side Up* quest).
2. Put a **chest right next to it**, that's where everything gets dropped off.
3. Right-click the pasture and add a Pokémon. The job it does depends on the species.
