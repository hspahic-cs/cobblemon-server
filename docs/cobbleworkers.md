# Cobbleworkers

Put your Pokémon to work. Park one on a Pasture Block and it'll auto-harvest, mine, or generate stuff for you while you're off doing other things.

## Setup

1. Place a **Pasture Block** (you get your first from the *Sunny Side Up* quest).
2. Put a **chest right next to it** — that's where everything gets dropped off.
3. Right-click the pasture and add a Pokémon. The job it does depends on the species.

## Range

Workers cover a **5×5 area** around the pasture (2 blocks out in every direction), and **5 blocks up and down**. Set your crops, target blocks, and chest within that zone.

## Tips

- **Exeggutor is the most flexible worker** — it can harvest both vanilla crops and Cobblemon berries, and it can sweep items. The quest chain after evolving one is built around getting it onto a pasture by a carrot patch.
- **One Pokémon does one job.** A Vaporeon won't harvest crops; an Exeggutor won't fill cauldrons. Match the species to the job.
- **A pasture holds up to 16 Pokémon** at once — stack workers if you want multiple jobs running in the same spot.
