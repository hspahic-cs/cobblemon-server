drop-only or loot-only, and competing uses for the same item. **Derived items are always ranked
above the ingredients they consume.**

### Crafting Chain Quick Reference

Derived items are always more valuable than their ingredients:

```
Hyper Training Candy > Exp. Candy XL > Exp. Candy L > M > S > XS
PP Max > Vitamins (6) > PP Up > Medicinal Brew > Medicinal Leek
Superb Remedy > Fine Remedy > Remedy > Big Root
Toxic Orb > Black Sludge (x2, drop-only)
Dragon Fang > Razor Fang (drop-only) + Dragon Gem (loot-only)
Eviolite > Everstone (drop/loot-only)
Choice Scarf > Silk Scarf > Normal Gem (loot-only)
Choice Band > Muscle Band
Choice Specs > Wise Glasses
Assault Vest > Protector (x2, also evo item)
Expert Belt > Black Belt > Fighting Gem (loot-only)
Rocky Helmet > Hard Stone (x3) > Rock Gem (loot-only)
Eject Pack > Eject Button
Loaded Dice > Iron Ball (x2)
Flame Orb > Fire Stone / Fire Gem
```

**Competing-use items** (extra valuable because demand exceeds supply):

```
Razor Fang: Dragon Fang craft + Gligar evolution
Protector: Assault Vest craft (x2) + Rhydon evolution
Everstone: Eviolite craft + breeding utility
Black Sludge: Toxic Orb craft (x2) + Poison-type held item
Eject Button: Eject Pack craft + held item
Type Gems: all type-boost crafting + finite loot supply
```
