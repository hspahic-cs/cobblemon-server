# Economy

> the design doc which has the full numbers; a friendlier walkthrough will
> replace this content.

The server runs two parallel currencies:

- **Coins** — your personal money. Earn from gym wins or selling items.
  Spend at the server shop or on the stock market.
- **BP (Battle Points)** — your faction's currency. Earn from faction gym
  challenges and events. Spend on faction upgrades and the faction cache.

Coins and BP **don't** convert to each other. They're separate systems.

## TL;DR

- Want a Potion or a Pokéball? **Coins.** Server shop, fixed prices.
- Want a Lucky Egg or Rare Candy? **Coins.** Stock market, dynamic prices.
- Want a Cherish Ball or an Ability Patch? **BP.** Faction cache only.
- Bet on a ranked match? **Coins.**
- Win a gym? **Coins** for personal-mode wins, **BP** for faction-mode wins.

## How fast do I earn?

Roughly **12 coins per minute** if you grind challenge-mode gym wins, or
**2 BP per minute** for faction gym work.
