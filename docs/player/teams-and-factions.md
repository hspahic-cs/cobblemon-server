# Teams & factions

> **Status:** stub — full guide pending.

Two layers of group identity:

| Layer | What it is | How you join |
|-------|-----------|--------------|
| **Team** | Valor (red), Mystic (blue), or Instinct (yellow) | First-login choice |
| **Sub-faction** | A named player group inside your team | `/faction create` |

A sub-faction belongs to exactly one team. You can be in a team without
being in any sub-faction — you can still earn BP from faction gym
challenges.

## What's the difference between a team and a faction?

- **Team** is for global cooperation: legendary attractor contributions,
  team-wide events, color identity
- **Sub-faction** is for small-group play: claim wilderness territory,
  build a faction gym, share a faction cache, raid other factions
